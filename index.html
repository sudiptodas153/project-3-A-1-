The Vision of Future

The vision for Bangladesh's future is to become a developed, high-income country by 2041. This vision is called "Vision 2041" or "Smart Bangladesh Vision 2041". The vision aims to make Bangladesh a prosperous, sustainable, and equitable country.

The Padma Bridge On The Country's Economy

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

The Padma Bridge, inaugurated as a milestone in Bangladesh's infrastructure, has profoundly impacted the nation's economy. By connecting the southwest region to Dhaka, the bridge has revolutionized transportation and reduced travel time significantly. This enhanced connectivity has boosted trade, facilitated the quicker movement of goods, and attracted both local and foreign investment.

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

The Dhaka Metro Rail system, officially known as the Mass Rapid Transit (MRT), marks a new era in urban transportation for Bangladesh. Designed to alleviate Dhaka’s notorious traffic congestion, the metro rail has become a symbol of progress and modernity for the capital city.
